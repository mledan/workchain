## üõ°Ô∏è Risk Mitigation

### Key Risks & Mitigation

#### 1. **Chicken-and-Egg Problem**
**Risk**: Need clients to attract freelancers and vice versa

**Mitigation**:
- Seed with own projects to create initial activity
- Partner with agencies for freelancer supply
- Content marketing to attract both sides simultaneously

#### 2. **Platform Competition**
**Risk**: Upwork, Fiverr have massive head start

**Mitigation**:
- Focus on differentiation (blockchain, lower fees)
- Target underserved micro-task segment
- Superior UX and integrations

#### 3. **Trust & Safety**
**Risk**: Fraud, scams, payment disputes

**Mitigation**:
- Escrow all payments
- Blockchain audit trail for disputes
- KYC for high-value transactions
- Insurance fund for edge cases

#### 4. **Regulatory Compliance**
**Risk**: Payment regulations, gig economy laws

**Mitigation**:
- Legal counsel from day one
- Compliance-first approach
- Work with payment processors who handle compliance

#### 5. **Blockchain Complexity**
**Risk**: Users intimidated by blockchain

**Mitigation**:
- Faux-blockchain MVP (no crypto required)
- Gradual migration to real blockchain
- Abstract complexity from users
